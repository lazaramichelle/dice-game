Game Rules

- The game has 2 players with an undetermined number of rounds
- During his turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as he likes. The dice total is added to the ROUND score.
- If the player rolls a 1, his ROUND score returns to zero and the player's turn ends.
- The player can choose to 'Collect' and end his turn to avoid rolling a 1.
- The game continues until either player reaches 100 or the number entered in the 'Final Score' input field.
